Team Top Guns Aero-Design NIT Jamshedpur

We are the aero-design team of National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur. We design and build radio-controlled aircrafts, and participate in the SAE Aero Design® competition, organized by Lockheed Martin and NASA every year in the United States.

The team was conceived in 2009. So far, we have built 6 aircrafts and have won the prestigious NASA Systems Engineering Award in 2011.
